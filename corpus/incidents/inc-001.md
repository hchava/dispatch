# INC-001 — user-events late arrival spike

## What happened
Kafka consumer was upgraded at 14:32 UTC. The new version changed the default watermark delay from 5 minutes to 0 minutes. Events with clock skew > 0 were dropped by the watermark filter instead of being held for the grace period.

Late arrival rate jumped from 0.8% baseline to 34% within 20 minutes of the deploy.

## Detection
Z-score anomaly detector fired on `late_arrival_rate` at 14:51 UTC (19 minutes after root cause). Threshold: > 3σ vs 30d rolling mean.

## Resolution
1. Identified the Kafka consumer config diff — watermark delay key changed name in the new version
2. Rolled back to previous consumer version at 15:19 UTC
3. Triggered replay of the dropped window (14:32–15:19 UTC) from the Kafka topic (retention: 7 days)
4. Verified late arrival rate returned to baseline within 2 windows

## Follow-up
- Added watermark delay to the regression test suite for consumer upgrades
- Added `late_arrival_rate` to the deployment health check dashboard
- Updated data contract to document the 5-minute grace period explicitly
